> *"So God created mankind in his own image, in the image of God he created them; male and female he created them."*
> — Genesis 1:27

## The Sentence That Defines You

Before the world told you what you were worth — before a grade, a paycheck, a relationship, a follower count — God said one sentence over you that nothing else can ever undo.

*In our image. In our likeness.*

It is the most honest thing ever said about a human being. You did not assemble yourself. You did not earn your dignity. You were spoken into existence by a God who decided, before you ever drew breath, that you would carry His image in the world. Every other identity you wear is small compared to this one. And every brokenness you feel is, at its root, the ache of an image that has been bent and is longing to be straight again.

This sermon is about that image — where it came from, what happened to it, and how Jesus gives it back.
